Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is iCanSearch for domain availability?
iCanSearch uses multiple API sources and cross-references results to ensure high accuracy. While we strive for 100% accuracy, we recommend verifying final availability with your chosen registrar before completing a purchase, especially for premium domains.
How many domain extensions can I search at once?
iCanSearch lets you search one domain at a time, but we automatically suggest alternative TLDs (Top Level Domains) if your desired domain is unavailable. You can quickly check these suggestions with a single click.
Does iCanSearch sell domains directly?
No, iCanSearch is a domain search tool that connects you with reputable domain registrars. We don't sell domains directly but provide links to trusted registrars where you can complete your purchase with confidence.
What does "premium domain" mean in search results?
Premium domains are high-value domain names that may be priced higher than standard registration fees. These could be short, memorable, or keyword-rich domains that have higher market value. When iCanSearch identifies a premium domain, we'll indicate this in the search results so you can make an informed decision.
Can I search for international domain extensions?
Yes, iCanSearch supports a wide range of international domain extensions including country code top-level domains (ccTLDs) like .uk, .ca, .de, and many others. Simply enter your domain name with the desired extension to check availability.
