Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers to some common queries.

How accurate is the domain availability check?

We strive for high accuracy by using real-time data from domain information providers like Domainr and supplementing with DNS checks. However, domain availability can change rapidly. Always confirm the status with the registrar before making any decisions.

What if the domain I want is taken?

If your desired domain is unavailable, iCanSearch provides a list of alternative suggestions, including different TLDs (like .net, .io, .co) or variations of your original name. You can also try a completely new name!

Does iCanSearch register domains?

No, iCanSearch is a domain availability checking tool. We provide links to popular domain registrars where you can purchase your chosen domain if it's available.

What does "Premium" or "Marketed" domain mean?

A "Premium" domain is often a short, memorable, or keyword-rich domain that registrars or resellers price higher due to its perceived value. "Marketed" means it's likely listed for sale on an aftermarket platform, often by its current owner. These domains are usually more expensive than standard registrations.
